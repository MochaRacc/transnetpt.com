Major works being conducted on the rail network

 

We're conducting major trackwork improvement and station refurbisment at Thornsbury, Welshpool, Stadium Park, Ashburton and all City Loop stations. We will be conducting these important works
at the least busiest time for the network. By making sure that we aren't making too much disruptions, we've selected a time period before the Easter break, meaning less disruptions and easy work times.

FAQ

When are the works happening

We're are planning to conductthese important works from 11:59pm April 6th to 11:59pm April 11th, we will be trying to work on bringing these services back where possible.

How will I be affected?

Check station signage for information

What is not permitted on Rail Replacement buses?

In compliance of the Public Transport (Compliance, complaints and miscellaneous) Act of 2004, skateboards, bicycles, uncaged pets and e-scooters are not permitted on our Rail Replacement buses
If you are found violating these rules, Transnet Officers can fine you up to $75,000 per violation.

Are the buses accessable?

All rail replacement buses operated by McKian's are accessable and disability friendly, make sure you look out for the accessible symbol
